# .github

Esync Deep

Esync Deep is a research and systems organisation focused on AI governance, structured intelligence systems, and trustworthy digital transformation.

We develop living research frameworks, applied governance models, and reusable system architectures for real-world AI adoption.

---

Purpose

This organisation exists to:

- Structure knowledge around AI systems and governance
- Publish living research and evolving frameworks
- Provide reusable models for safe AI adoption
- Bridge research, policy, and implementation

---

Core Areas of Work

1. AI Governance Systems

Frameworks for safe, auditable, and transparent AI deployment.

2. Trust & Risk Modelling

Structured approaches to evaluating AI reliability, risk, and impact.

3. Adoption Frameworks

Real-world patterns for integrating AI into organisations and public systems.

4. Living Research

Continuously evolving publications rather than static documents.

---

Key Repository

Beyond AI Disruption

A living research specification exploring AI governance, trust systems, and structured adoption models.

This is the primary publication and reference point for Esync Deep research output.

How to Navigate This Organisation

Start here:

1. Read the pinned repository: "beyond-ai-disruption"

Versioning Philosophy

We use a living-document model:

- v1.0 → Initial publication
- v1.x → Iterative refinement
- v2.0 → Structural evolution

No document is final. All knowledge evolves through structured updates.
